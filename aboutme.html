About Me
"A Man With A Plan"

Welcome to the Grand Gibson Hotel! If you’re here looking for the work of Kevin Finley, then you’ve come to the right place. The Grand Gibson acts as a main hub for all my online presence from forums and modding communities to podcasts and articles.

There’s a lot to be found here at The Grand Gibson Hotel, feel free to look and dive into what it has to offer, take a tour around and learn about its history and future while viewing my work. Whatever you do, enjoy your stay and do come again.

The video package above is a small trailer that I composed for the cornerstone piece in my graduation portfolio. The project is a mod for The Elder Scrolls: Skyrim dubbed Tales From Tamriel. The mod was designed to feature a fully playable quest with voiced characters that would span over several downloadable installments. Feel free to learn more and uncover the secrets by viewing it from the “Projects” sections.

If you haven’t noticed already; there’s much more here than just game mods and videos. About 95% of everything on this will have something to do with the game industry as that is my focus but if you’re into digital entertainment in general, I’m sure there’s something here for you to enjoy!
