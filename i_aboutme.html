Information/About Me

What do I do?

I craft narratives. Interactive stories are a series of events working together to give players a unique experience. My art does not rely on just dialogue, cinematics, or words to tell a story; it pulls together nearly every aspect of a gameâ€™s design to make the story breathe.

Each piece of a game, from a level to a game mechanic, is a building block capable of adding immersion to the narrative. I use those blocks to engage players' minds and give them moments they can talk about. With an emphasis on diegetic design, I aim to create living, compelling, engaging narratives that everyone can take a unique experience from.

What do I know?

Wanting to understand every element of design, I amassed a considerable list of skills including (but not limited to) a working knowledge of AP style writing, effective blogging formats, level design with some basic scripting, sound 3D modeling foundamentals, and HTML5, which I used to make this very site from the ground up.

Why Do It?

I discovered video games late in my childhood, but when I found them, I fell in love with them. I began craving immersive experiences, craving my next escape into worlds where I could do what I want. I love interactive narratives and I love making unique stories for players to experience. I craft narratives and I love what I do.
