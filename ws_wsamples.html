Sample Writing - Anime Challenge - Warriors of Algia

Tasked with creating a synopsis for an anime cartoon, I came up with Warriors of Algia. A world set in medieval times is besieged by a vengeful divine who gets stronger by the number of his devotees. In order to survive, the world Algia must rely on a ragtag group of young strangers, each blessed and championed by a divine.
